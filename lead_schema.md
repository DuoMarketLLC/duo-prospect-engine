# Lead Schema

This defines what a "lead" looks like in the DUO Prospect Engine.

Each lead should include:

## Basic Info
- Business Name
- Website URL
- Industry (CPG, Retail, Service, etc.)
- Location (City, State)

## Contact Info
- Contact Name (if available)
- Email (if available)
- Phone (if available)

## Business Signals
- Has Website (Yes/No)
- Website Quality (Low / Medium / High)
- Active on Social Media (Yes/No)
- Selling Products (Yes/No)

## Qualification
- Likely Needs eBroker (Yes/No)
- Likely Needs BidCloser (Yes/No)
- Likely Needs BidRescue (Yes/No)
- Likely Needs Plug & Play Marketer (Yes/No)

## Scoring
- Lead Score (1–100)

## Notes
- Observations about the business
- Why this lead is a good fit
